# **App Name**: FreshStep Clean

## Core Features:

- Service Display: Showcase a comprehensive list of carpet cleaning services, including descriptions of the methods used, benefits, and types of carpets handled.
- AI-Powered Instant Quote Tool: An interactive tool where users can input details like room size, carpet condition, and specific needs to receive an immediate estimated service cost.
- Online Booking Request Form: A form allowing customers to request a service appointment, providing their contact information, preferred date/time, and specific service needs.
- Before & After Gallery: A visual showcase of successful cleaning projects, demonstrating the quality of work with 'before' and 'after' photographs.
- Customer Testimonials: Display positive reviews and testimonials from satisfied clients to build trust and credibility.
- Contact & Location Information: Clearly display business contact details, operating hours, and the geographical service areas.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A refreshing and trustworthy teal-green (#1F9E82), symbolizing cleanliness and reliability, used for primary calls to action and important elements.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, subtle greenish-gray (#F2F7F5) to create a clean, airy, and inviting canvas.
- Accent color: A lively, brighter green (#7EE59D), analogous to the primary, used sparingly for highlights, secondary interactive elements, or success indicators.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a modern sans-serif with a clean and professional appearance, for titles and headings.
- Body font: 'Inter', a highly readable sans-serif, ensuring clarity and legibility for all service descriptions, testimonials, and forms.
- Utilize clean, outline-style icons related to cleaning, freshness, and home services for a contemporary and easily understandable visual language.
- Employ a minimalist and spacious layout with generous white space to enhance readability and create a sense of order and cleanliness.
- Subtle fade-in and slide-up animations for content sections as the user scrolls, creating a smooth and engaging browsing experience without being distracting.
